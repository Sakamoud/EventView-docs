Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 5, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Eventiva collects minimal information to provide you with the best experience:

  • Events You Create: Custom events you add are stored locally on your device.
  • AI Chat History: When you use the "Ask Horizon" AI feature, your questions and responses are processed through our secure backend.
  • Purchase Information: If you subscribe to Premium, Apple handles all payment processing. We only receive a confirmation of your subscription status.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Display upcoming events and holidays
  • Provide AI-powered insights about events through OpenAI
  • Manage your Premium subscription status
  • Improve app performance and features

3. Data Storage

Local Storage: All your events and preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage systems.

AI Processing: When you use the AI chat feature, your queries are sent to our secure backend server and then to OpenAI for processing. We do not store chat histories on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

Eventiva uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI: Powers the AI chat feature. Subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • Calendarific: Provides holiday data. Subject to Calendarific's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple In-App Purchase: Handles all payment processing. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • All communication with our backend uses HTTPS encryption
  • API keys are secured server-side and never exposed in the app
  • We implement rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • No personal information is collected beyond what's necessary for app functionality

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete Your Data: Use the "Delete All Data" option in Settings to remove all locally stored information
  • Reset Your Account: Use the "Reset App" option to start fresh
  • Cancel Subscription: Manage your Premium subscription through Apple's Subscription settings

7. Children's Privacy

Eventiva does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
